Description

Discover the versatility of my multi-store application, where innovation meets convenience. Seamlessly crafted with Flutter, this application revolutionizes the shopping experience by offering distinct sections for both suppliers and customers. Users can log in, explore a variety of payment options including Razorpay, Cash on Delivery, and PayPal, and conveniently manage their profiles with access to past orders. The intuitive design allows users to add products to wishlists or carts effortlessly. On the supplier side, access detailed statistics, view orders, and explore a marketplace featuring products from other sellers. Join the journey of streamlined shopping and efficient retail management encapsulated in this dynamic multi-store application.
