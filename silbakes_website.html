Description

Silbakes is a local bakery based in Kochi, specializing in freshly baked, custom cakes for various occasions such as birthdays, weddings, and special events. The website I created for Silbakes serves as an online showcase of their delicious cake offerings, providing an intuitive and visually appealing platform for customers in the Kochi area to explore their products, place orders, and get in touch with the bakery.
